## How much does Degenerator cost?

The following fees apply when minting a token:

1. Solana Network fees: Creating the mint accounts and signing the transaction ≈ 0.005 SOL

The following fees apply when creating CPMM pools on Raydium:

1. Solana Network fees: Creating the pool accounts and signing the transaction ≈ 0.002 SOL

2. Creating the LP token account (ATA)\*: 0.00203928 SOL

3. Protocol Fee: 0.15 SOL is collected for CP-Swap (CPMM) pools during pool creation to deter pool spamming and ensure protocol sustainability.
